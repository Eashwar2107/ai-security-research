# Level 8 — The Final Boss

**Attack:** Not sharing this one.  
**Defense:** Everything.

---

Level 8 combines everything the previous levels taught you. There's no single trick that works — you need to think across the full attack surface.

If you're stuck, here's how to approach it:

**Review what's worked so far**
Every bypass in levels 1–7 exploited a gap between what the model was *told* to protect and what it actually protected. Level 8 is the same — find the gap.

**Things worth trying**
- Techniques that worked on earlier levels, reapplied differently
- Combining two approaches from different levels into one prompt
- Changing the *format* of your request, not just the wording
- Thinking about what the defense is *actually* checking for — then asking for something adjacent

**What not to waste time on**
- Brute force / random prompts — this level requires deliberate thinking
- Asking directly in any form — it's airtight on direct requests

**The mindset**
You're not trying to break the AI. You're looking for the assumption the developer made when writing the defense — and exploiting that assumption.

Good luck. It's worth it.
